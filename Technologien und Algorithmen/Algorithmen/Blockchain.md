### Technical Features
- Cryptographic Hash functions
- Timestamped appended-only logs (Blocks)
- Block headers & Merkle Trees
- Asymmetric cryptographic & digital signatures
- Addresses
- Consensus through either:
	- Proof of Work
	- Proof of Space
	- Proof of Stake
- Network of Nodes
- Transaction inputs and outputs
